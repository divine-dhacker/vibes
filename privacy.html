Privacy Policy

At GhostRoom, your privacy is our core product. We have engineered this platform to collect as little data as possible.

1. Data Minimization

GhostRoom does not collect names, phone numbers, or email addresses. We do not use traditional account systems. All identifiers are stored locally on your device or associated with your temporary session.

2. Ephemeral Data Handling

Messages and media links are stored in a real-time database only for the duration of the room's life. Our automated "Sweeper" protocol ensures that every byte of data associated with an expired room is permanently wiped from our production servers 24 hours after creation. We do not keep archives or backups of your private conversations.

3. Advertising & Analytics

To keep GhostRoom free and anonymous, we partner with Klipy for monetization.

  • Klipy IDs: When you use stickers, an anonymized ID is sent to Klipy to facilitate ad payouts and prevent fraud.
  • Ad Delivery: Klipy may serve contextually relevant advertisements within the sticker picker. No private chat data is ever shared with advertisers.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to monitor app performance and crash reports, using anonymized IP addresses.

4. Data Security

We use SSL/TLS encryption for all data in transit. While we implement industry-standard security measures, no digital platform is 100% secure. By using GhostRoom, you accept the inherent risks of online communication.
